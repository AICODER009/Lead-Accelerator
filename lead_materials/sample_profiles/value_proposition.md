# B2B Lead Accelerator Value Proposition

Our software, Lead Accelerator, helps B2B tech and service businesses automate their outbound lead generation using advanced agentic AI.

## Key Benefits
- **Hyper-Personalization**: Adapts pitch angles automatically based on lead profiles and company press releases.
- **Objection Simulation**: Runs pre-flight simulations on email copies to stress-test response strength before sending.
- **SQLite Checkpointing**: Resumes outbound pipelines instantly after review or pauses.
- **JSON-RPC Agent Networking**: Connects specialized copywriting services dynamically to ensure clean delivery.

## Target Audience
Founders, CEOs, CMOs, and VP Sales at B2B companies (especially SaaS and IT services).
